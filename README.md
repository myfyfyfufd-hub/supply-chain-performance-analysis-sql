# Supply Chain Performance Analysis Using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes supply chain operations using SQL and MySQL Workbench. The objective is to evaluate inventory management, supplier performance, transportation efficiency, manufacturing costs, shipping operations, and revenue generation through SQL-based business analysis.

The project transforms raw supply chain data into meaningful business insights that support operational efficiency and strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations rely on efficient supply chain management to reduce costs, improve customer satisfaction, and maximize profitability. Poor inventory planning, inefficient transportation, high manufacturing costs, and quality issues can negatively impact business performance.

This project uses SQL to analyze supply chain data and identify opportunities for operational improvement.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyze overall supply chain performance.
* Evaluate revenue generated by different product types.
* Measure supplier performance.
* Monitor inventory levels.
* Analyze shipping and transportation efficiency.
* Evaluate manufacturing costs.
* Identify products with high defect rates.
* Generate business insights for decision-making.

---

## Business Questions

1. What is the total revenue generated?
2. Which product type generates the highest revenue?
3. Which suppliers contribute the most products?
4. Which transportation mode is used most frequently?
5. Which shipping carrier processes the most orders?
6. What are the average manufacturing costs by product type?
7. Which products have the highest defect rates?
8. Which SKUs generate the highest revenue?
9. Which products maintain the highest inventory levels?
10. Which inspection result occurs most frequently?
11. What is the average lead time?
12. Which customer demographic generates the highest revenue?
13. Which suppliers have the highest manufacturing costs?
14. What is the average shipping cost by carrier?
15. Which supply chain areas require optimization?

## Key Findings

* Revenue varies significantly across product categories.
* Certain suppliers consistently contribute more inventory.
* Manufacturing costs differ among product types.
* Transportation modes have different operational impacts.
* Some products exhibit higher defect rates than others.
* Inventory distribution highlights opportunities for stock optimization.

---

## Recommendations

* Prioritize inventory management for high-revenue products.
* Monitor suppliers with higher manufacturing costs.
* Reduce defect rates through stronger quality control.
* Optimize transportation routes to lower shipping costs.
* Improve forecasting to maintain efficient stock levels.
